Our Story

Founded in 1985, ToyWorld Manufacturing began as a small family workshop with a big dream: to create toys that would inspire generations of children to learn, imagine, and grow.

Today, we are a global leader in toy manufacturing, but our commitment to quality, safety, and innovation remains the same. Every toy we produce goes through rigorous testing to ensure it meets the highest standards of safety and durability.

At ToyWorld, we believe play is a child's most important work. Our toys are designed to support healthy development while providing hours of entertainment and joy.

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our toys and manufacturing

Are your toys safe for children?

Absolutely! Safety is our top priority. All ToyWorld toys undergo extensive safety testing and comply with international safety standards including ASTM F963, EN71, and ISO 8124. We use non-toxic materials and design our toys to be age-appropriate, with no small parts for toys intended for children under 3 years.

What materials do you use in your toys?

We use a variety of high-quality, durable materials including sustainable wood, non-toxic plastics, organic cotton, and eco-friendly dyes. All materials are carefully selected to ensure they are safe for children and kind to the environment. We're committed to increasing our use of recycled and biodegradable materials across our product lines.

Do you offer custom toy manufacturing?

Yes! We work with businesses, educational institutions, and organizations to create custom toys and educational materials. Whether you need a small batch of specialized items or a large-scale production run, our team can help bring your vision to life. Contact our custom manufacturing department to discuss your project needs.

Where are your toys manufactured?

Our main manufacturing facility is located in Edison, New Jersey. We're proud to maintain local production that supports our community while ensuring the highest quality standards. We also have smaller production facilities in Europe and Asia to serve our global markets, all operating under the same strict quality and ethical guidelines.

How do you ensure ethical manufacturing practices?

Ethics is at the core of our business. We ensure fair wages and safe working conditions for all employees. Our facilities are regularly audited by independent organizations to verify compliance with labor standards. We also work closely with our suppliers to ensure they adhere to our strict code of ethics and sustainability practices throughout the supply chain.
